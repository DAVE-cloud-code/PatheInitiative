The Future Pathway Program

The Future Pathway Program is a youth development initiative designed to help secondary school students discover their potential, build confidence, and prepare for future opportunities in education, leadership, and career development.

The program provides mentorship, career guidance, leadership training, and practical learning experiences that equip participants with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed both inside and outside the classroom.

At its core, the Future Pathway Program exists to help secondary school students gain clarity about their future and develop the confidence to pursue their goals with purpose.

About the Program

The Future Pathway Program creates opportunities for students to learn, grow, and explore different pathways for their future.

Through interactive classes and career talks, participants are encouraged to think beyond limitations and become more intentional about their personal and academic growth.

The program focuses on helping young people develop the mindset, skills, and exposure needed to thrive in the university.

What the Program Offers

  • An inspiring university-style course, taught by NYSC Corpers through a series of seven tutorials, culminating in a challenging final assignment.
  • A chance to build the knowledge, skills and confidence to apply to the most competitive universities.
  • The opportunity to learn about higher education and life at university at a Graduation Event.

How are Students Supported?

For some students, the only doctor they may have ever met is in a medical setting, but our programme opens up the world of academic research and gives young people the opportunity to study university-level work in an accessible way.

Our tutors are recruited from the National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) with a range of disciplines covering STEM, arts and humanities and social sciences.

Each student receives a course workbook to support them through the programme and also gains access to our online portal for learning support.

PATHE Partner School Benefits

  • A dedicated Program Officer to support schools.
  • A detailed impact report showing student progress.
  • Regular newsletters for schools and students.
  • University guidance and educational resources.
  • Mentoring resources for scholars.

Why Tutor With Us?

  • Receive expert teaching and pedagogy training.
  • Gain sustained teaching experience.
  • Develop communication and leadership skills.
  • Join a nationwide community of tutors.
  • Receive competitive pay and benefits.
  • Boost your CV and future job opportunities.

How Does It Work?

Once onboarded as a Tutor, you provide your availability before each school term. The PATHE Team will then place you in a school where you will teach a small group of students weekly.
